Custom multi-rater feedback architecture designed for boardrooms and C-suite teams. We combine quantitative metrics with clinical-grade interviews.

Gathering Objective Leadership Feedback Without Friction

At the senior level, honest feedback is scarce. Direct reports often filter critical truths, and peer groups operate under cooperative diplomacy. A structured, confidential 360-degree assessment provides officers with a precise mirroring of their impact.

We do not rely on standard check-the-box software surveys. Our process merges quantitative Likert indicators with qualitative partner-led interviews to capture subtle nuances.

Diagnostic Elements Included

Hogan Assessments

HPI (Day-to-day traits), HDS (Derailer tendencies), and MVPI (Core motivations and cultural alignment drivers).

Qualitative Interviews

Partner-led confidential sessions with directors, peers, and direct reports to unpack operational habits.

Blind Spot Mapping

Cross-referencing self-evaluations with peer feedback to map cognitive blind spots in leadership style.

Execution Roadmap

Actionable, concrete developmental suggestions directly aligned to individual commercial deliverables.
