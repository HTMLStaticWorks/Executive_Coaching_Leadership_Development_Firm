The Aurelius Way

Our Methodology

We merge organizational strategy, behavioral science, and board-level risk management into a timeline-based framework that yields measurable change.

Phase I

Strategic Discovery & Board Briefing

We begin by aligning with the Board of Directors or key executive sponsors. We review the corporate strategic goals, timelines, and transition parameters to establish strict objectives.

Phase II

360° Diagnostic Assessment

Deploying Hogan Leadership inventories and clinical qualitative 360-degree stakeholder interviews to understand operational blind spots, strengths, and behavioral derailers.

Phase III

Roadmap Architecture

We draft a personalized, milestone-driven growth plan that connects behavioral modifications directly to key business metrics (e.g. M&A timelines, capital raises).

Phase IV

Advisory Summit Iterations

Ongoing monthly 1:1 C-suite counseling sessions, leadership simulations, and real-time boardroom coaching to build and enforce critical execution traits.

Phase V

Metrics Audit & Hand-off

Re-running diagnostic indicators, measuring strategic goal accomplishment, and handing off executive briefs to governance committees.
